Research Focus

Core research areas

Condition Monitoring

Intelligent condition monitoring using vibration analysis, acoustic signal processing, oil analysis, and sensor-based diagnostics for early fault detection and predictive maintenance of industrial machinery.

Artificial Intelligence

Development of AI-driven engineering solutions using machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and intelligent decision-support systems for mechanical engineering applications.

Fuzzy MCDM

Advanced fuzzy multi-criteria decision-making models for engineering design, material selection, maintenance planning, risk assessment, and industrial optimization.

Smart Manufacturing

Research on Industry 5.0 technologies, intelligent manufacturing, digital transformation, smart factories, and sustainable engineering systems.
